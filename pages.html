WE CARE ABOUT HOW OUR KIDS LEARN

To us, how kids learn is as important as what they learn.

Besides science, kids learn self-esteem.

Nutty Scientists® shows and workshops stimulate the imagination and spirit of children, arousing their curiosity and fostering an environment where they are encouraged to participate, boosting their self-esteem.

To us, how kids learn is as important as what they learn.

Nutty Scientists has always prided itself on doing things a little differently. In fact, we dare say that no one else in the world teaches quite like we do. Our programs draw from the expertise of renowned scientists from all corners of the globe.

The Nutty Scientists Methodology

Nutty Scientist create fun, hands-on, spectacular interactive activities in the form of shows or workshops led by our professional staff of Nutty Scientists. By utilizing our internationally recognized and awarded pedagogic methodology, the children become active participants and receive exciting and memorable educational experiences.

This methodology uses various educational techniques:

  • TPR© (Total Physical Response) Method
  • SDAIE© (Specifically Designed Academic Instruction in English)
  • PQS© (Plonsky Question System)
